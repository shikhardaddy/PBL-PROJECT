AI-Driven Predictive & Sensor-Assisted Intelligent Traffic Signal System

A hybrid AI system that predicts traffic congestion using historical data, behavioral patterns, Google Maps integration, and weather APIs, while using minimal low-power sensors for real-time validation.

Problem Statement

Traditional traffic signals operate on fixed timing cycles regardless of traffic density. This causes long waiting times during low traffic and severe congestion during peak hours. Pure sensor-based systems consume high power and require heavy maintenance. There is a need for a predictive, energy-efficient, intelligent traffic management system.

Literature Review

Existing systems rely on fixed timers or heavy real-time sensor usage. Most lack predictive modeling using long-term behavioral and API-integrated data.

Research Gap

Our approach combines AI-based prediction with selective sensor validation, balancing efficiency, sustainability, and accuracy.

System Methodology

Dataset / Input

Historical traffic data, Google Maps API, Weather API, government databases, and IoT sensor validation data.

Model Architecture

LSTM, Regression & Random Forest models for traffic prediction with hybrid control architecture.
