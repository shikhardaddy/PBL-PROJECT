AI-Driven Predictive & Sensor-Assisted Intelligent Traffic Signal System

A hybrid AI system leveraging historical datasets, behavioral modeling, Google Maps integration and minimal IoT validation to dynamically adjust traffic signals for sustainable smart city mobility.

Addressing Urban Congestion

Traditional traffic signals operate on static timing cycles, causing inefficiencies during both low and peak traffic hours. This leads to fuel wastage, pollution, and unnecessary delays. Our system introduces predictive intelligence to proactively manage traffic.

  • • Long waiting times during low traffic periods
  • • Poor handling of peak hour congestion
  • • No integration of weather or events
  • • High manpower & energy waste

Conventional traffic lights lack adaptability needed for modern cities.

Core Goals of the Intelligent System

Predict Traffic Patterns

Use historical datasets & ML models (LSTM, Random Forest) to forecast congestion trends.

Analyze Mass Behavior

Model office hours, festivals, tourism seasons, and large-scale commuting patterns.

Dynamic Adjustment

AI suggests optimal signal durations before congestion occurs.

Two-Layer Hybrid Architecture

Layer 1: Predictive AI Engine

Trained using historical traffic logs, event patterns, weather data, and behavioral trends. Integrates Google Maps API and government data to forecast optimal signal durations.

Layer 2: Sensor Validation

Low-power IoT sensors validate predictions. Only activated when deviation exceeds threshold, ensuring energy efficiency.
